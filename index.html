How does Rankeet Work?

We know all about your closest pitches

Did you know there are more than 30K sports courts in Spain? We can identify your closest ones and tell you if there are any spots available for you.

We plan to expand our technology so that you can improve your stats anywhere!

Identify yourself and play football!

In all our pitches you can identify yourself as a player with NFC technology implemented in both goals. Each team player will identiy themselves by putting their phones close to the sensor.

Once identified, just play! Feel free to use any other device connected to the app, like a smart watch. Every step counts!

Let the street rank you

Do you want to see your stats? Keep playing and improve your stats thanks to our technology. After some healthy competition, compare yourself to others and improve your social time!

You can see other players who are similar to you so that you can build the most balanced team. Improve your football experience!

Frequently Asked Questions

01 Can I schedule a match in one of your pitches?
Of course, just sign in and choose one of our pitches. If there is a spot available at any time, choose it and you will be signed up for that match!
02 How does the app know that I really played?
Before the match, every player must go to our NFC area and log in. Just open our app when you are close and it will tell you what to do :).
03 Can I rank my own team?
You can only rank some players after every match. This is made to avoid having invalid stats in our app. However, our organizers can rank you guys if he saw something special in you, so be creative!
04 How do I improve my stats?
You can improve your stats by showing up to the matches and trying to be the best team player the street has ever seen! Remember to log in with NFC so that you don't lose your match stats!
